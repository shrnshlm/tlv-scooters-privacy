TLV Scooters — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

What we collect. TLV Scooters does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

Location. If you grant location permission, your device’s location is used only on-device to center the map. It is never sent off your device, never stored, and never shared.

Network requests we make. To show vehicles and zones, the app fetches:

  • Public real-time scooter data from Lime, Bird, and Dott (GBFS feeds).
  • Address autocomplete from OpenStreetMap’s Nominatim service.
  • Zone overlays from tlv-scooters.vercel.app.

These services receive standard HTTP request metadata (your IP address, User-Agent). We do not control their privacy practices; please review their respective policies.

Third-party tracking. None.

Children. The app is not directed at children under 13.
